Beyond Dark

By: Mortal Mind Games - Niagara College

Project

Beyond Dark is the graduation project of Mortal Mind Games, the team developing the title, 4 months for development the project will be launched in the LevelUp Event.

Game

Beyond Dark is an upcoming first-person action horror game where the player will embody Vlad, an enthusiast of dark magic that lives in a medieval world. Certain events have brought him to an abandoned looking village which was known for the dark magic practiced in it, in the village the player will find himseld trapped by an evil entity that wanders around leaving them trapped within. To escape the village the player will find Relics to become powerful and have an attempt at defeating the entity in a different dimension.

My work was focused on the art and implementation to the engine, scripts where provided and only partially adjusted.
